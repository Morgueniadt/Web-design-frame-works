Designing Clear Path: A Journey Towards Mental Wellness

Our thoughtful process focuses on creating an intuitive and soothing app for mental health management.

Our Design Process

1. Research & Ideation

We started by understanding the mental health needs of users, focusing on features like mood tracking, journaling, and meditation. Research included surveys, user interviews, and analysis of existing apps.

2. Wireframing

Wireframes were created to visualize the app layout and user flow. We focused on structure and usability, ensuring a seamless interaction with the core features of the app.

Initial Wireframe for the Main Screen

3. Design Iterations & Final Design

Throughout the process, we iterated the visual design, testing different color schemes, fonts, and user interface elements. The design went through several rounds of adjustments, ensuring we achieved the right balance of simplicity and usability.

Conclusion

The Clear Path app's design process was a collaborative journey of research, ideation, and iteration. Our goal was to craft an app that enhances mental wellness through simplicity, user-centered design, and intuitive features.
